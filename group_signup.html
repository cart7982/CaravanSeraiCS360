Group Sign Up

Create a group to allow for greater trading capacity

Groups allow for the use of resources collectively; that is, each user who joins the group allows their items to be used by any member of the group.
To join a group, enter the name of the group and the group password.
If you do not have these things, please contact the creator of the group.
